Click on a bubble to pick it up. Click on a box to move it there but be aware your bubble can't move along diagonals and needs a free path to reach it's target. Try to get Five or more in a row, column or diagonal to make them vanish from the board. But be careful, every time you move a bubble and don't connect Five or more your enemy places three new bubbles on the board.
